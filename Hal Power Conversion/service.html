ANNUAL MAINTENANCE CONTRACT

At Hal Power Conversion, we know how critical reliable power is to your business. That’s why our Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) ensure your UPS, inverters, and batteries stay in top condition all year round.

Our AMC includes scheduled preventive maintenance, where our skilled technicians perform thorough inspections and tests to catch issues early, preventing costly downtime. We also provide firmware and software updates and use advanced tools to monitor critical components like batteries, helping to predict and prevent failures.

Each visit is documented, giving you clear reports on your equipment’s health and future needs. With our customized service and expert team, you can rely on us to keep your power systems running smoothly and your business uninterrupted.
